# HAL YieldOS — Market Positioning

## Core Product Identity

HAL YieldOS is not an AI model.

HAL YieldOS is a read-only Functional Yield Evidence Layer that converts imperfect industrial
system data into EvidencePacks, Functional Passports, and Decision Readiness reports for
human review and AI-assisted workflows.

Short position (English): **AI-ready Functional Yield Evidence Layer**

Short position (Korean): **기능수율 기반 산업 증거 계층**

Core statement: **AI speaks. YieldOS produces evidence. Forge remembers. Humans decide.**

한국어: AI는 말한다. YieldOS는 증거를 만든다. Forge는 기억한다. 사람은 결정한다.

---

## What This Is

HAL YieldOS converts imperfect industrial system data into functional-yield evidence packages
for human review and AI-assisted decision workflows.

한국어: HAL YieldOS는 불완전한 산업 시스템 데이터를 기능수율 EvidencePack으로 바꿔,
사람 검토와 AI 보조 의사결정에 사용할 수 있게 만드는 소프트웨어다.

It is not a control system, a yield optimizer, or an AI autopilot. It is a read-only
shadow analysis tool that produces Functional Passports — structured evidence documents
that require human review and approval before any operational decision.

## What It Complements

YieldOS complements:

- Industrial AI platforms (by providing structured, evidence-lineage-preserved input)
- Engineering review workflows (by producing candidate-only evidence for human sign-off)
- Yield analytics (by classifying functional roles, not just pass/fail)
- Failure analysis (by preserving evidence chains, not certifying root cause)
- Human decision review (by producing Decision Readiness reports)
- Forge decision memory workflows (future: via Forge-compatible export)

---

## Value Proposition

Binary pass/fail policies discard hardware that still has functional roles. YieldOS identifies
those roles and produces evidence for human engineers to review. The Functional Passport
documents what remains functional, what is blocked, and under what conditions the analysis holds.

This does not guarantee the hardware is safe to use. That determination belongs to human
engineers with domain authority.

## Overclaiming Boundary

YieldOS does not claim:

- Certified root cause analysis
- Guaranteed yield improvement
- Production-proven reliability
- Autonomous recovery capability
- AI model behavior

YieldOS claims only:

- Evidence collection from telemetry
- Functional role reclassification as a candidate
- Decision readiness assessment (not decision authority)
- Audit trail for human review

## Functional Yield Organizing Principle

YieldOS does not merely organize industrial data.
YieldOS creates functional-yield evidence about remaining functions, blocked functions, valid conditions, and supporting evidence.

Every artifact in the output bundle exists to answer:

**What can still function, what must be blocked, under what valid conditions, and based on what evidence?**

YieldOS is not a generic observability platform, not AI middleware, and not a workflow automation system.
It is an AI-ready Functional Yield Evidence Layer.
